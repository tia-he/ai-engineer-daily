# AI Engineer Daily Roadmap

This roadmap tracks the development of AI Engineer Daily from MVP to a production-ready product.

---

# Vision

Build a daily AI & software engineering briefing that helps developers stay informed in just a few minutes every day.

Instead of overwhelming users with dozens of articles, AI Engineer Daily provides:

- curated news
- AI-generated summaries
- technical explanations
- trustworthy sources
